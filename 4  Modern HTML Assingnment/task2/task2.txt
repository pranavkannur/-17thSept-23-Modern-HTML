HTML entities are used to display reserved characters, special characters, or invisible characters in 
HTML These are characters that might be confused with HTML code or are not present on a normal keyboard.
example,
        1] Non-breaking space: &nbsp; or &#160;
        2] Less than sign: &lt; or &#60;
        3] Greater than sign: &gt; or &#62;
        4] Ampersand: &amp; or &#38;
        5] Double quotation mark: &quot; or &#34;
